# Molas — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Personal Kanban board with swimlanes (tracks), task cards, goals, checklists, dependencies, calendar, and time tracking. Used alone, daily, to organize personal and professional work.

## Users
Solo user (the creator). Uses on desktop, bright ambient light, Portuguese speaker. Wants a tool that feels premium and intentional — not generic SaaS.

## Register
product

## Brand tone
Clean, focused, slightly opinionated. Not bubbly. Not corporate. Not "startup template."

## Anti-references
- Generic white Notion clone
- Washed-out pastel productivity tools
- Glassmorphism cards with frosted blur everywhere
- Rainbow-gradient text
- The standard "blue SaaS" palette

## Strategic principles
- Dark mode is the primary design target (black/white luxury aesthetic, already done)
- Light mode must feel equally intentional — not just "dark mode inverted"
- Personality comes from contrast and restraint, not decoration
